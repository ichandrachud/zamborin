About Zamborin Games

Zamborin Games is a small independent studio building free, instantly-playable games for the browser. Nothing to download. No login. No accounts. Just open a game and play.

What we make

Hand-crafted browser games that respect your time — quick to start, friendly on mobile and desktop, easy to share. Each game is built with vanilla HTML, JavaScript and Canvas — no engines, no bloat. The whole catalogue is a single static site that loads fast on any connection.

  • Empyrean — a free-flight WWII dogfight over hand-painted skies, plus an ocean blockade and a desert raid
  • Tessera — an endless falling-letter word puzzle
  • Untangle — a daily planar-graph puzzle
  • Carrom — the South-Asian tabletop classic, three difficulty levels
  • Ludo — race four tokens home before three AI opponents

How we work

The studio runs lean. Every game is small enough to be built and shipped end-to-end by one or two people. We add new games regularly. When a game doesn’t work, we cut it. When one resonates, we expand it.

How we make money

The games are free and always will be. The site is supported by a small number of advertisements served via Google AdSense. Visitors from the EU, UK, and Switzerland get a consent banner the first time they visit; everyone else sees ads without the prompt. See our privacy policy for details.
