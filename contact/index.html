Contact

The fastest way to reach us is by email.

then_and_again@hotmail.com

What to email about

Bug reports (especially anything that crashes a game or breaks on a phone). Feature requests for an existing game, suggestions for a new one. Press, partnership, or licensing enquiries. Or just a hello.

Response time

Zamborin Games is a small team — replies usually go out within a few days. Travel periods may stretch that to a week. Mark genuinely-urgent messages “urgent” in the subject and we’ll prioritise.

Privacy

When you email us, we’ll see your name and address as displayed in your mail client and whatever you write in the body. We use that information only to reply. We don’t add you to any list. See the privacy policy for the full story.
